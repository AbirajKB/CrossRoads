About Us

                  Packapeer Academy is founded in 2019 by Nikhil Kilivayil along with his brother Sibi Kilivayil with an aim to help millions of students across the globe to improve their standards of living by educating them with industry relevant skills. The Academy has started in a small district of Calicut with less than 12 students and has grown into a reputable institution in the hearts of millions of people across Kerala.
                Packapeer Academy has operations in 3 major cities of Kerala i.e Calicut, Kochi and Trivandrum and has impacted hundreds of students as of now. Packapeer Academy has two different programs. 1) SPS-School of Practical Studies or SPS is the brand name under which Packapeer Academy conducts its technical training. 2) Crossroads-Crossroads is our YouTube channel where we constantly broadcast useful and informative content related to technology and career building. Crossroads has crossed 20K plus subscribers and eight thousand plus watch hours on YouTube. Nikhil is also one of the board members and the current President of a Technical NGO called Ping Foundation which focuses on building Open Source projects that have social commitments and imparting technical education to the rural youths.
